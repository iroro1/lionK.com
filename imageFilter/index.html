About

This app is created using the Duke Learn to Program Js Library from the Programming foundations course.

This app allows users to upload a picture and apply the color filters to the picture. This course has increased my interest in image processing and i intend to make additional research in that field as soon as possible.

I have learnt about the green screen problem, manipulating images and most interestingly Steganography which was a part of the honours program. The idea of Steganography is to hide data in images using simple Math. Utilising base 2 numbers or even decimals, you can hide images under other images or even text under images.

I hope i have been able to show through this little image processing app and other apps on my portfolio what i can do as regards Coding.
